returns

After 30 days, product must be unworn and unwashed to be returned. Flawed or defective product purchased online can be returned if manufactured less than two years ago. Learn more. Read your product's label, hang tag or warranty card for for specific guarantees or warranties. Once received, we'll process your return and issue a refund to the original payment method.

All products purchased on Nike.com must be returned online. Start your return above. Products purchased at a Nike retail store or authorized Nike retailer, must be returned to the store. Learn more. We're unable to exchange online purchases. Start a return above to return your purchase.
